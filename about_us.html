ABOUT IISc

Indian Institute of Science is a public institute for research and higher education in science and engineering. IISc was established in 1909 and claims a large, green campus in Bangalore, Karnataka. It continues to remain one of the best institutes in the world for scientific research. It was ranked first in the NIRF rankings for four consecutive years and as of 2020, is in the top 200 list of the QS rankings. The UG course with a heavy emphasis on an interdisciplinary syllabus was introduced in 2011. IISc, with its distinctive heritage, skilled community and past and present research projects is an active contributor to the fields of science and engineering.
