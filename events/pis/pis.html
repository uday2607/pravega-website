PIS

Description of PIS:- An event where a few teams (mainly students), after rigorous rounds of selection and evaluation, are chosen to present their ideas for a start-up - a proposal that is either research oriented or approaches a problem with a commercial objective in mind. The competition is open to everyone and teams must submit drafts of their proposals. These are then examined and after scrupulous judging on the basis of the relevance, scientific value, innovation and clarity of the ideas presented, teams are selected for further rounds. Finalists have the opportunity to discuss their ideas with the judges and receive insightful feedback and suggestions on their project, making P.I.S a true learning experience.
