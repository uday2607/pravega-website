Talk 1: The Quantum Way

By Prof. Arindam Ghosh

  • Date and time 5:00 PM, 22nd August
  • YouTube link Will be updated soon

Abstract: The speaker shall outline some of the excitements in technological impact of quantum physics, and give an outlook towards a new generation of computation, communications and sensing.

Bio: Prof. Arindam Ghosh is an experimental condensed matter physicist and a Professor in the Department of Physics, Indian Institute of Science. His areas of expertise include low-dimensional systems and nanostructures. He obtained his PhD from IISc and did his post-doctoral research at Univ of Cambridge. During his tenure at IISc, he was also a Visiting Research Fellow in Nanotechnology at T J Watson Research Center of IBM, Yorktown Heights, New York, USA. He was awarded the Shanti Swarup Bhatnagar Prize for science and technology, the highest science award in India.

Talk 2: Searching for the fundamental building blocks of the universe at the Large Hadron Collider

By Prof. Jyothsna Komaragiri

  • Date and Time 4:00 PM, 23rd August
  • YouTube link Will be updated soon

Abstract: IISc has started the Experimental High Energy Physics program in 2016 and since October 2016 IISc has been part of the Compact Muon Solenoid (CMS) experiment at the Large Hadron Collider (LHC), CERN. In this talk, Prof Jyothsna will cover the basics of Particle Physics and give an overview of how a typical search for particles is performed at the CMS experiment at LHC.

Bio: Prof, Jyothsna Komaragiri is currently an assistant professor in Center for High Energy Physics(CHEP) at Indian Institute of Science(IISc) Bangalore. She has an experience of working at three high energy physics world-wide collaborative experiments, namely Compact Muon Solenoid (CMS), A Toroidal LHC Apparatus (ATLAS) (both at CERN) & DZero(at Fermilab).
