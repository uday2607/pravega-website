Decoherence '21 is here!

Spooky quizzes

Quizzes will be conducted online in two rounds: theoretical and experimental. These are timed quizzes based on UG level physics. The top five performers (on the basis of both quizzes) will be appearing for a subjective round, where participants have to find solution(s) to the given question(s) over a period of 18 hours. This will take place once in every 3 weeks

(Details about prizes yet to be decided)

Coherence lecture series

We will be streaming live lectures delivered by physicists, experts in their fields, talking about exciting topics. These lectures will be followed by a QA session. This will take place once in every 3 weeks
