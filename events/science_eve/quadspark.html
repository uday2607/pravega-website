Quadspark

Pravega 2020 invites young geniuses of India to its school quiz competition, Quadspark and compete with other students from other metropolitan cities. We will organise one offline prelim and the selected participants will get a chance to come to Indian Institute of Science for finals. We hope to see great enthusiasm in school kids from 8th to 10th standard. So, pull your socks, put on your hats and register for the event now!

  • Category : Science
  • Coordinators : Ishan Bhat & Kartikey Pratap Chauhan
  • Prelims on : 31st January 2020 at 10 AM in J.N Tata Auditorium, IISc
  • Reporting Time : 9:30 AM
  • Contact Details : ishanbhat@iisc.ac.in
