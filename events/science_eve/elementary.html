Chemenigma presents,

Elementary

Elementary aims to probe creativity and critical thinking in students and enthusiasts in Chemistry. The online event shall involve thought-provoking questions related to classical as well as evolving fields in the extensive discipline of Chemistry every week. Participate and answer multiple questions posted by us each week (days to be updated in advance) and compete to rake points and maintain your position on the leader-board! The event is planned to span four weeks, so make sure you hold your position at the top!

Additionally, we shall host monthly webinars where participants get to learn about new and upcoming fields of research in Chemistry from eminent chemists around the world! So mark your calendars and visit our website as well as the official Pravega handle. For regular updates via email, do subscribe to our newsletter using the form below.

Awards

The top three participants shall be awarded attractive cash prizes!

17th August onwards
