BioBlitz


The Pre-Pravega segment of BioBlitz aims at popularising the various frontiers of biology. The event shall include a monthly mixed bag of MCQs, crossword puzzles and so on. Besides these monthly problem sets, biweekly HOTS questions, focussing primarily on the expanding horizons of the biological sciences, shall also be released to tease your gray cells, fire them up and tremendously increase the currents flowing through those long tailed cells! The problem sets (both the biweekly HOTS questions and the monthly mixed bag) shall be released here on this website and through the Pravega Instagram handle at the scheduled date.

Apart from these quizzes and problem sets, there shall be monthly webinars addressed by eminent biologists from all over the globe, so that the audience can get to hear ‘straight from the horse’s mouth’ - the doyennes of the respective fields, who are actively probing into natural systems, unravelling the mystery of life and creating knowledge. It just can’t get any better! Do visit this site and the social media handles of Pravega for more updates on the webinars. In case you want to receive regular email updates about the problem sets or the webinars, subscribe to our newsletter using the form given at the end of this page.

Awards

The winners of the monthly quizzes and the biweekly problem sets shall be given Instagram shoutouts from the official Instagram handle of Pravega! Apart from this, a leaderboard of the contestants shall be maintained, and the top few contestants shall be given lateral entry to the final event scheduled to be held in IISc (at a date to be decided later), without having to pass through the Prelims! This is thus a great opportunity to secure your entry to the finals to be held in IISc! The top three candidates shall be awarded e-certificates as well!

Date

15th August onwards

Eligibility

Open to all. The only criteria to participate in the event is to possess active interest in biology!
