Hurray! The most awaited Pravega 2020 is back again! Reaching out to all the drama and theatre lovers out there.
Your beloved Proscenium is again embellished for your Grace this year.
Proscenium is an open stage theatre competition where participants across the nation get to portray their acting skills and theatrical talents. Various expertise in this field will be judging the performances and will be present to give you suggestions and help to get better in your skills.
This time, alongwith STAGE THEATRE we include MIME ACT as well.
Participate and get a chance to win exciting prizes worth INR 45,000.
