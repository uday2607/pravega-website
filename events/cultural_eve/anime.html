Animathon

A city wide anime quiz open for all ages jointly organised by Japan Habba and Pravega. Also features a performance by the well-known cosplay band Daisuki. Following last year's success this edition will feature multiple new rounds. Join in the excitement and form teams of two. Participants from all disciplines are invited for the throne of the anime quiz champion and prizes worth 15,000 INR. The quiz is hosted by Hosted by Animaniax, the anime group of IISc and sponsored by Japanese Language Teachers' Association, Bangalore (バンガロール日本語教師かい BNK).

  • Venue - JN Tata Auditorium, IISc
  • Date and Time - 1 February 2020 (Saturday) 2.30PM-6PM
  • Category - Cultural
  • Coordinators - Ruman Malik & Shubham Acharya
