Lasya Presents

In Sync

What’s poppin people? Lasya is back to fill joy to your feet!

This time around we want you to create your own Hook step for a Hook beat and add more appeal to the song. So show us what you got and put your best foot forward!

Rules

  • Pick a song from the list we provide.
  • Perform your hook step to the song.
  • Email your hookstep video to us at pravega.lasya@gmail.com (You can also post it on all your social media platforms)
  • The video should not exceed 30 seconds.

Awards

The winners will be selected under two categories:

  1. Pravega’s choice
  2. People’s choice (based on your hookstep's popularity on instagram)

2 winners will be announced in each of these categories.

Pravega's choice

  • 1st Prize Rs.600 + Social media Shoutout + E-certificate
  • 2nd Prize Rs.400 + Social media Shoutout + E-certificate

People's choice

  • 1st Prize Rs.600 + Social media Shoutout + E-certificate
  • 2nd Prize Rs.400 + Social media Shoutout + E-certificate

There will also be Friday shoutouts to the performer of the week, every week during the tenure of the event.
