Comedy act by Karunesh Talwar

Comedian after dark, part-time day dreamer and full-time rebel Karunesh Talwar is not a fan of gainful employment. One of the most honed acts in the country today, Karunesh has performed in pretty much every city and on all stages; big, small and weird. He also writes for television because integrity is nice but money is better. His wit will have you rolling on the floor laughing!

Masala Coffee

Formed in 2014, and soaring high in the Indian music industry ever since, Masala Coffee has been winning hearts with their beautiful covers of various Malayalam, Hindi and Tamil songs. They made their debut on Kappa TV’s Music Mojo in 2014, performing their own unique versions of popular Tamil songs like “Munbe Vaa” and “Snehithane”. Videos of their performances went viral and the band soon achieved widespread fame through social media. Pravega presents you with the chance to catch them live headlining the biggest Battle of Bands in the country on the 1st of February!

Pineapple Express

Coming back to Pravega at IISc after 2018 is this Indian progressive fusion band. The love that Bengaluru has for them is no secret. Having performed countless shows in South India, Pineapple Express will be here at IISc to deliver yet another unforgettable evening. Pineapple Express set themselves apart by their unique songwriting and racemic blend of several styles of music including carnatic, rock, electronic and djent. Their characteristic sound is evident in their EPs, ‘déjà vu’, ‘Uplift’ and other singles.

  • 7:30 to 9:30
  • Gymkhana

Monuments

All the way from the UK with three banging albums under their belt is a band ready to blow away your senses. Equipped with songs from their latest studio album, Phronesis, featuring hits like ‘A.W.O.L’, ‘Leviathan’, ‘Stygian Blue’ and more, Monuments will be performing live on 2nd February, 2020 - for a truly catalytic finish to India’s largest science fest.

Monuments

Monuments consist of Andy Cizek (vocals), John Browne (guitar), Olly Steele (guitar), Adam Swan (bass), and Mike Malyan (drums, samples). They have been touring all over the world performing for ecstatic fans everywhere. After seven long years, they will be performing for the second time in India, to a crowd that is sure to be as passionate as ever.

Event description

Pravega 2020, sponsored by Airbus and powered by Canara Bank, presents to you Pronites. The day 3 of Pravega Pronites will be headlined by Monuments and their guests Pineapple express, putting an end to the biggest science and cultural fest of India.

We have Karunesh Talwar and Masala coffee to entertain you and add more taste to these racemic evenings, on Pravega Pronites day 2. Pronites comprises of two major events spread over the two days of Pravega. The first Pronite is a standup show by the likes of artists like Rahul Subramanium and Biswa Kalyan Rath, followed by Battle of Bands and a headliner. On the last day of Pronites, Pronites hosts an electrifying concert by leading international bands like Tesseract and Intronaut.
