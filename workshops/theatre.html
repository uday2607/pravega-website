Theatre Workshops

The Unlearning

The Unlearning is about discarding all preconceived notions and myths about theatre and stage-acting from one's memory and explore and learn the basics of theatre. The workshop is an introductory course that aims to arouse interest in the intricacies of the theatre experience, shed inhibitions and teach participants stagecraft. This workshop is unique since it focuses on bringing out the artist and nurturing him. It is designed for participants with or without any background in acting.

Improv Theatre

The workshop will cover the basics of improvisational theatre. Participants will undergo exercises which teach then how to be free on stage mentally and physically. The various formats of Improv theatre will be discussed and differentiated, and the various philosophies improv theatre artists use to perform improvisational theatre.
